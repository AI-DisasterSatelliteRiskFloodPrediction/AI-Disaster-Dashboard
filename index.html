Integrating AI-Driven Satellite Image Analysis into National Disaster Risk Reduction Strategies

This project uses Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Earth Observation data to improve flood and landslide early warning systems in Rwanda.

Research Overview

The system combines Sentinel-1/Sentinel-2 satellite imagery, Google Earth Engine processing and AI models to detect and predict disaster risks.
