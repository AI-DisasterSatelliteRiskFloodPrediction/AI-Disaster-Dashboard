Research Overview

This research project explores the integration of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Earth Observation satellite data to improve disaster risk monitoring and decision-making. The system transforms satellite imagery into actionable information through automated hazard detection, risk mapping and interactive decision-support dashboards.
