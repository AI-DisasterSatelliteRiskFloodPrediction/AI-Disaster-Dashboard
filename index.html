Research Overview

This research project explores the integration of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Earth Observation satellite data to improve disaster risk monitoring and decision-making. The system transforms satellite imagery into actionable information through automated hazard detection, risk mapping and interactive dashboards.

Research Workflow

Satellite Imagery → Artificial Intelligence Models → Flood Risk Prediction → Decision Support Dashboard

Research Focus

  • Satellite-based flood monitoring
  • AI-driven disaster prediction
  • Climate resilience technologies
  • Remote sensing applications
  • Early warning systems
